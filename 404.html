We have no idea but the page you are trying to access does not exist. This might be because you have entered the web address incorrectly or the page has moved. Or maybe your intellectual curiosity brought you here.

Use mental models below to find your way back.


  • Tragedy of the Commons: This page is really popular among everyone but the sustainability of this page is at jeoppardy because no one wants to maintain it.

  • Game Theory: By not viewing this page you help everyone else get better pages.

  • Supply and Demand: Demand for the 404 page is at very low and supply is very high.

  • Moral Hazard: Showing you this page would only encourage you to want more pages.

  • Natural Evolution: This page is constantly evolving based on environmental changes.

  • Pareto Principle: Spend 20% of your time on the home page to get the best 80% results. There is nothing here.

  • Diminishing Returns: One more unit of time spent on this page and you will experience decrease in marginal output.
